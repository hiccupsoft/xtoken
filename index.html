Terminal frees up dev hours and minimizes risk

The problem

Projects are spending scarce developer resources building financial primitives that are outside of their core competencies – time that could be spent on their actual product. This results in inefficiencies and UX tradeoffs while introducing risk.

Our solution

We provide permissionless financial infrastructure to projects that they can leverage without any development or financial expertise. Our apps are designed to be seamless and secure, built on a best-in-class contract architecture and intuitive UI.

The Terminal difference

End-to-end experience

Manage the entire lifecycle of each solution within the app. For example, with Terminal Mining, projects won't need to visit the Uniswap front-end or manage LP tokens.

Breadth of solutions

Use Mining to configure and deploy incentivized liquidity mining programs in minutes

  1. Configure liquidity

    Choose your liquidity pool and set your program's price range. Deploy a new Uniswap V3 pool if necessary.

  2. Set rewards structure

    Choose the rewards token(s) you want to pay out to LPs. Decide whether rewards should be vested.

  3. Initialize rewards

    Deploy a rewards program with custom amounts and duration.

Use Origination to launch a custom token sale in a few easy clicks

  1. Configure offering

    Choose the offer token and purchase token. Set the total sale and reserve amounts.

  2. Choose sale parameters

    Set sale duration and pricing formulas (ascending / descending auction or flat price) for whitelist and public sales.

  3. Set vesting schedule

    If desired, configure vesting and cliff periods for the sale.
